About Us


We believe everyone should have the opportunity to make a difference in the lives of people and society. That's why we make it easy for good people to connect with good cause. Mid-City Gives Back is a fictitious non-profit organization which helps organizations find volunteer activities in the metro area.


Started in 2012, Mid-City Gives Back hosted various events in collaboration with several non-profit and service oriented organizations. We at Mid-City Gives Back encourage every individual to give some of their time to the society. This portal helps the kind hearted find organizations that host various volunteer events and the current volunteering members to track their hours of service.
